Templatizer editions

Choose a template when you scaffold a new Rails app. The Carbon edition uses file-driven layouts and the render-tree engine so additional design systems can follow the same pattern. Source of truth for automation is templates/registry.json.

Edition Design system Command
Default Rails Modern Tailwind utility UI (inline generator heredocs) ./templates/rails-modern/create_rails_app.sh MyApp
Rails Carbon IBM Carbon–aligned (Gray 100 palette, Plex, glyph shell) ./templates/rails-carbon/create_rails_app.sh MyApp

Render engine

  • scripts/engine/render-tree.sh copies templates/rails-carbon/files into the new app and replaces __APP_NAME__, __APP_NAME_LOWER__, __APP_NAME_CLASS__, and __APP_DISPLAY_NAME__.
  • Add new editions by copying the Carbon layout, pointing template.json at a new files/ tree, and invoking the same engine from templates/.shared/create_rails_app.sh.
